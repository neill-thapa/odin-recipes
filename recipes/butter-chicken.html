Recipe of Butter Chicken

Description

Butter Chicken, or Murgha Makhani, is an iconic North Indian dish with juicy, marinated chicken cooked in a rich, creamy tomato-butter sauce. It's mildly spiced, making it a favorite for all ages, and pairs perfectly with naan or basmati rice.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • ½ cup cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, chili powder, turmeric, and ginger-garlic paste for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat butter in a pan, sauté onions until golden, add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  3. Add marinated chicken and cook until chicken is tender and fully cooked.
  4. Stir in cream and garam masala, simmer 5 minutes.
  5. Add cooked lentils, simmer on low heat for 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
